Some viruses use RNA rather than DNA as their genetic material. The current statement therefore concerns cellular organisms and DNA-based viruses, not every biological replicator.

The 1944 experiment was pivotal, but acceptance was not instantaneous. Additional experiments and the elucidation of DNA's structure helped consolidate the new understanding.
