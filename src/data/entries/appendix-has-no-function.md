In evolutionary biology, “vestigial” does not necessarily mean entirely functionless. A structure can be reduced from an ancestral role while retaining or acquiring other functions.

The appendix is not indispensable, and some proposed microbiome functions remain under investigation. The correction is to the absolute claim that it does nothing.
