The term “prokaryote” remains useful as a descriptive label for cells lacking a nucleus, but it does not denote one simple evolutionary branch equivalent to Archaea or Bacteria.

Modern phylogenomic research continues to refine the deepest branches of life, including the relationship between Archaea and Eukaryotes.
