Wave-particle duality is not the claim that a photon is literally a tiny classical ball that sometimes transforms into a water-like wave. Quantum objects are described by a framework whose measurement outcomes can exhibit both kinds of behavior.

Classical wave optics remains highly accurate for many macroscopic applications.
