This card rejects an absolute prohibition, not every narrower claim about the human brain. Adult neurogenesis is well established in several animal models, while human hippocampal neurogenesis remains methodologically contentious.

New neuron production is also not the brain's only form of plasticity. Existing neurons continually alter their connections and function.
